Privacy Policy

BrainBubbles mobile application

Last updated: 19 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how BrainBubbles ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as screens visited, session duration, and basic performance data.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, language settings, and anonymised identifiers for analytics (for example, Apple-provided identifiers).

The App is not designed to collect sensitive personal information such as your real name, address, or payment card details. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details.

2. Children's Privacy

BrainBubbles may be used by children under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected personal data from a child, please contact us and we will delete it.

We use the information we collect to:

We may use third-party services such as analytics and crash reporting tools provided by Apple or other providers to help us understand how the App is used and to improve stability and performance. These providers may collect anonymised usage data and device information as described above.

5. Data Storage and Retention

We retain data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law. Where possible, data is stored in aggregated or anonymised form.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct or delete your data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address below. We may ask you for additional information to verify your identity.

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. We take steps to ensure that data is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
