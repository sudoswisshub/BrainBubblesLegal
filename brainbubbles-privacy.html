Privacy Policy

BrainBubbles mobile application

Last updated: 19 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how BrainBubbles ("App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the App.

1. No Personal Data Collection

BrainBubbles is designed for children and families and does not collect, store or share any personal data.

In particular, we do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses or contact details;
  • Create user accounts or profiles;
  • Track individual users across apps or websites;
  • Collect location data;
  • Collect any data that can be used to identify you or your child.

2. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or social media services.

There are no ads and no third-party SDKs for analytics, crash reporting, or marketing inside the App.

3. On-Device Activity

Game progress and settings, if any, are stored locally on your device. This information stays on the device and is not transmitted to us.

4. Purchases

If the App offers in-app purchases, all payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details or any billing information. Any purchase data is handled solely by Apple in accordance with their terms and privacy policy.

5. Children's Privacy

BrainBubbles is suitable for children and is built with privacy in mind. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not knowingly store any information relating to children.

If you believe that the App is collecting personal information in a way that is inconsistent with this Policy, please contact us using the email address below so we can review and take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information for us to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions about privacy in relation to BrainBubbles, you can still contact us and we will be happy to clarify how the App works.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if the functionality of the App changes. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we ever introduce features that involve collecting personal data, we will update this Policy and, where required, request your consent.
